Lentils

Lentils are a healthy alternative to beef

Ingredients:

  • 6 cups water
  • 6 Ounces dry lentils
  • 1 Onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves, garlic
  • 1 tablespoon, paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 15 cumin seeds, or more to taste, ground
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps:

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Stir lentils into water, reduce heat to low, and cook at a simmer, stirring occasionally, until the water is mostly absorbed, 25 to 25 minutes.
  3. Stir onion, garlic, paprika, bay leaf, cumin, salt, and pepper into the lentils;
  4. continue cooking until the water is fully absorbed and the lentils tender, 5 to 10 minutes more. Stir olive oil into the lentils.
  5. Remove and discard the bay leaf.
