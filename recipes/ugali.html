Ugali

Ugali made from maize flour is a staple food in East Africa.

Ingredients:

  • Maize flour
  • 2 Litres of water
  • Pinch of table salt
  • 1 teaspoon mixed spices

Steps:

  1. Heat the water in a cooking pot until boiling point
  2. Add the salt and the spices to the boiling water and stir
  3. Using a half litre cup, set aside some of the boiling water
  4. Add the maize flour into the cooking pot as you stir using a cooking stick
  5. Continue stirring the water/flour mixture until it acquires a spongy texture
  6. Use the spare water from the cup to balance the texture as desired
  7. Reduce the heat, cover the Ugali and let it simmer for five minutes
  8. The Ugali is ready, serve on a banana leaf or plate as desired
  9. Enjoy your Ugali!
